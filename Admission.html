ADMISSION INFORMATION

We admit students across the globe into Junior High(Years 9-11) and Senior High(Years 10-13) Classes. For the secondary school, prospective students must be at least nine(9) years old at the time of Admission.

ADMISSION PROCESS

The following will guide parents/guradians through the admission process. Please read through and folllow the procedures:

  1. Purchase an admission form at the cost of =N=5,000.00 per form.
  2. Forms could be obtained from the college and the Junior School.
  3. Have passport picture and National Population birth certificate ready.

ENTRANCE EXAMINATION

Subjects to be written for admission into both the junior senior Secondary Schools include the following:

  1. English
  2. Mathematics
  3. General Paper

FEES

  1. Fees are to be paid to the bank before the end of the second week of resumption.
  2. All tellers obtained from the bank upon payments of the approved fees must be presented of the Bursar for proper documentation and receipts.
