LIFE AT SMART SCIENCE COLLEGE

ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES

Our Academic process is broken down into series by the Ministry of Education Nigeria, so that impacting of knowledge into students can followed to give our best. We make sure no student in the school is less than the average score so to maintain a nice learning environment, but if that happens based on a resonlble condition, the student is meant to take that class again or other wise.

BOARDING HOUSE

We do have a good boarding house with nice facilities. Parents are encourage to use this if they live outside calabar. There is a matron and a sit-in night teacher in the hostels.

SAFETY AND SECURITY

At Smart Science College, the security and well being of our students is of utmost improtance to us. For this reason we have well trained out-sourced security outfit that patrol the schools' perimeter both day and night.

EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

At Smart Science College, we know that every student's forte lies in academics; nothing is wrong with that once it is recognized. The many icons today did not necessarily excel in academics e.g., David Branson, etc. So we therefore encourage students to join clubs and to participate in sports.

The extracurricular programme is also desingend to expose students to the real world. Some include;

  1. JETS Club for those that are intrested in advanced science.
  2. Maths Club for those intrested in mathematics
  3. Conservation Club for those who care about the world
  4. Road Safety Club
  5. Singing Club
  6. Debating Club
  7. First Aid Club
  8. Reading Club
  9. Scrabble/Chess Club
  10. Dance Club
  11. Craft Club

FACILITIES

SCIENCE LABORATORY

We have nice and well equiped labratories for practical works during the learning session, the laboratories ranging from the physics lab, chemistry lab, biology lab and craft lab. At the laboratory the students safety is also our concern therefore all our scince staffs are areaapproprriately trained on safety and a wide range of safety equipment

COMPUTER LABORATORY

We have nice and well-maintained Computers with internet services.

MUSIC ROOM

The music room was built for those that have talents in music and are intrested in using that talents, although this is a science school there are some students that are intrested in music as an elective subject

LIBRARY

We have a well-stocked library (rated 'A' by the outreach Abuja Inspection Team). The comprehensive stock of books meets not only the academic needs of students, but also encourages their leisure reading.

TECHNICAL DRAWING STUDIO

Our Technical Drawing studio is a well-equipped one it was rated 'A' by T.D Inspection Team
